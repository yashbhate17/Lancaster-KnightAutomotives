Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our vehicles and services.

What types of vehicles does Lancaster & Knight Automotives offer?

We offer a diverse range of vehicles including sedans, SUVs, hatchbacks, and electric vehicles from renowned brands like Kia, Volkswagen, and Tesla.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we collaborate with multiple financial institutions to offer flexible financing solutions tailored to your needs.

Can I schedule a test drive online?

At Lancaster & Knight Automotives, we believe in providing a personalized experience for our customers. To ensure this, we currently offer test drives by appointment at our dealership. We invite you to visit our showroom or contact us directly to schedule a convenient time.

What warranty comes with the vehicles?

All our new vehicles come with a standard manufacturer warranty. For used vehicles, we offer certified pre-owned warranties. Specific details vary by model.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the contact form on our website, by calling our helpline, or by visiting our dealership during business hours.
