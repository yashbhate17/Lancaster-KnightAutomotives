About Lancaster & Knight Automotives

Company History

Founded in 2025, Lancaster & Knight Automotives began with a vision to redefine the automotive experience in India. Over the time, we've expanded our presence across Maharashtra, introducing innovative services and building a reputation for excellence in both sales and after-sales support.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to deliver exceptional automotive solutions with unwavering integrity and a commitment to customer satisfaction. We value:

  • Excellence: Striving for superior quality in all we do.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and transparency.
  • Customer Focus: Prioritizing our clients' needs and experiences.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas to enhance our services.
